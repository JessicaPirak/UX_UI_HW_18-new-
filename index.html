Nice to Meet You!

Hi, I’m Jessica, an emerging UX/UI designer with a deep love for video game
interfaces and a burning passion for digital art. Welcome to my world, where I blend
the realms of design and illustration to create immersive experiences.
Video games have always been my playground, capturing my imagination and fueling
my creativity. As a UX/UI designer, I've found my niche in crafting interfaces that
enhance the gaming experience and bring players closer to their virtual adventures.

My creative journey doesn't stop at UX/UI design. In my free time, I indulge in the art
of illustration. I find joy in bringing characters and worlds to life through digital
drawings. It's a way for me to unwind, explore new styles, and express my creativity
outside the realm of design.

Beyond my professional pursuits, you'll find me exploring the vibrant worlds of video
games, discovering new art styles, and seeking inspiration in the digital realm. It's a
constant reminder of the boundless possibilities that await us as designers and artists.

My Process

1

User Research

  • Identifying the problem.
  • Empathize with users to understand behaviours, motivations, goals, and pain points.
  • Conducting interviews, surveys, and usability testing, to gain insights.
2

Definition

  • Problem statement.
  • Gathering and organizing the results from the research.
  • Creating the user persona to represent target audience.
  • Creating a value proposition for stakeholders.
3

Ideation

  • Brainstorming ideas based on “how might we” statement with a number of matrixes.
  • Completing a competitor analysis.
  • Creating storyboards, user scenarios and/or user journey maps.
4

Prototyping

  • Creating the user flow to visually represent how the users will complete their goals.
  • Designing lo-fi wireframes to gain insight on how users will interact with design.
5

Testing and
Iterations

  • Conducting user tests to gather feedback and insight on how users interact with prototype.
  • Making iterations based on test results.
  • Designing final Hi-Fi prototype.
